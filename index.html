Welcome to Peony Pals!

Ever wanted some flowers that you can say YOU made? Welcome to Peony Pals. At Peony Pals we have our customers give us a reason they need a bouquet, and other customers join to gether and form an idea. This idea is then submit to the Peony Pals staff and we make your pals idea a reality for your loved one.

The Blooming Concept

If you have an idea of your own, but no reason to make the flowers, then you can submit your ideas to our webpage so that others can use your idea for their reasons. This will be under the titles "Peonies" in our browser.

Designer Templates

We've crafted some gorgeous templates for your ideas to bloom into Peonies, and shere them with your Pals.

Rather Make Yourself a Peony?

If you'd prefer to make a Peony for yourself, you can do that by clicking "Make Your Own" and your idea will be on its way.
